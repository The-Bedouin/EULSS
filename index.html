About EULSS

The Elizade University Law Students Society (EULSS) is the heartbeat of our law faculty—empowering students through events, advocacy, and community service since its founding in 2010.

Our mission is to nurture leadership, legal excellence, and lasting connections that extend far beyond Graduation, EULSS is your platform to grow and give back.
